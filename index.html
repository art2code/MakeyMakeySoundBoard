MakeyMakeySoundBoard

This is just a simple sound board to test Makey Makey keyboard and mouse actions in your browser.

Audio is implemented with howler.js

How it works ?

Simply download the source code, extract and run index.html in your favorite browser.

Turn up the sound

Play with your makey makey
or test it with your mouse and keyboard:
left click and letters w / a / s / d / f / g / h / j are supported.

Enjoy !
